CANADA

Canada, the second-largest country in the world by land area, is celebrated for its vast wilderness, diverse culture, and friendly people. Spanning from the Atlantic to the Pacific and stretching into the Arctic, Canada offers a stunning array of landscapes, from the Rocky Mountains to serene prairies and vibrant cities. With two official languages, English and French, Canada embraces multiculturalism and is home to a rich Indigenous heritage. Cities like Toronto, Vancouver, and Montreal are known for their dynamic arts scenes, innovative industries, and culinary delights. Canada's emphasis on outdoor activities, including hiking, skiing, and canoeing, makes it a haven for nature enthusiasts.
